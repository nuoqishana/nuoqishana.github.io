GUI Design

Chinese Card是一款对外汉语学习软件，帮助初学汉语的外国人更加快速高效的记住中文单词。用户在进入系统页面后，可通过右上角的选项按钮，针对自己水平选择相应的词库：
基础词汇 | 应用词汇 | 高级词汇 | HSK等级考试词汇
在每个词库内，有若干的list，用户可以根据自己的学习进度逐一学习。点击开始以后，每个单词以卡片形式显示，用户在学习完某个单词以后，将该卡片往左滑动，即可出现下一个新单词。在出现已经掌握的单词时，用户只需将该卡片向上划出，此单词则不会再出现在该轮的学习中。
在学习每个list的过程中，会随机出现对单词掌握情况的小测试，该小测试的时间倒计时方式为界面背景上由左往右的蒙版进度条（如上图所示），在不影响用户注意力的情况下，提醒其注意答题的时间。
在完成每个list的学习后，系统会自动分析出用户对于该list内单词的掌握情况，用户可以根据自身情况选择“再次复习”或者是“继续学习下个list”。

Product Design

Problem Identification

A cotton swab always carries a large number of bacteria or virus when we finish the treatment in our daily life. Without professional medical recycle devices, these unhealthy cottons, if disposed unproperly, often lead to secondary pollution. Moreover, in some less developed countries and regions where is suffer from natural disasters, the number of using the disposable swabs could be much more great. If these cotton swabs come to the garbage recycling system, undoubtly, it will lead to a consequence of epidemic disease.

Solution Concept

Swab Cover is a device to cover the used cotton tips, and sterilize them automatically. After the treatment, you can fold the swab easily due to the stress concentration groove. Then, tear up the surface of swab cover, and stick one side to another. This device is low-priced to mass-produced while can effectively prevent the spread of disease, especially in regions where large amounts of people need the traumatism treatment. Moreover, swab cover can be used in families to make life more bioclean.
